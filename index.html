Student Symposium 2025

February 20th to 21st

Preamble

The First edition of the Annual Students' Symposium was held in the academic year 1987-88. The novel initiative was formulated by Gouthama, presently a faculty member at IIT Kanpur. Traditionally the aim, with which this event was envisaged, was to create a platform for the students of the department to interact by presenting and sharing their research amongst themselves. With time, and with the induction of undergraduates, this event has broadened to include the new members of the department, as well as try to familiarise the students with the research done elsewhere. This year, we take pride in ourselves by maintaining the legacy of this Annual Students' Symposium for 38 years.

Message

Students' symposium serves as a platform for students to showcase their research, share insights, and engage in meaningful discussions across various disciplines. It is a testament to the intellectual vibrancy within our academic community. This year's symposium promises to be a unique and enriching experience. The diverse range of presentations and discussions will undoubtedly contribute to the expansion of our collective understanding and inspire future breakthroughs. This symposium is not just an event; it is a dynamic forum for the exchange of ideas, fostering a spirit of inquiry and discussions. The interactions and knowledge shared during this symposium will have a lasting impact on our academic journey. The 38th Annual Students' Symposium will be held on 20th and 21st February 2025. It gives us immense pleasure to be a part of the organizing team of the students' symposium. We hope this gathering be a source of inspiration, learning, and camaraderie for all.

Forum

The symposium serves as a dynamic platform for researchers across the diverse fields of materials science, including functional materials, computational materials, metallurgy, biomaterials, and polymers. Through featured talks, poster presentations, and discussions, this event fosters collaboration among students, researchers, and professionals, offering an opportunity to showcase discoveries and engage in meaningful dialogue. By bridging the gap between academia and industry, the symposium invites prominent speakers from globally renowned organizations to create an enriching experience.
