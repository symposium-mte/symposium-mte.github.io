About Us

We are proud to host the 38th Annual Student Symposium on Metallurgical and Materials Research. Established during India’s early independence, our department evolved from Metallurgy to Materials Engineering, leading advances in metals, nuclear materials, biomaterials, polymers, and nanotechnology. This symposium helps students showcase their research to an audience with diverse backgrounds and receive constructive feedback. We encourage you to share your research, grow professionally, and make this event a memorable success.
